Dune: Imperium

A game of galactic conquest and political intrigue

Overview

Dune: Imperium is a game of galactic conquest and political intrigue set in the Dune universe. Players take on the role of one of four factions vying for control of the planet Arrakis, the only known source of the valuable spice melange.

The game combines worker placement, deck-building, and combat to create a unique and engaging experience. Players must carefully manage their resources, build their armies, and form alliances to achieve their goals.

Worker Placement

Place your workers on the game board to collect resources, build units, and develop your faction's abilities.

Deck-Building

Customize your deck of cards to gain new abilities and strategies. Use your cards to outmaneuver your opponents and achieve your goals.

Combat

Lead your armies into battle to conquer territories and gain control of Arrakis. Use your units' unique abilities to outmaneuver and defeat your opponents.
